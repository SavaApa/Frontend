History of the origin of the universe

How did the Universe we know come into being? And how do we explain its origin? Undoubtedly, all the other evidence and data collected over the years by cosmologists indicates that it all could have started with a "big bang." But what if there is more? In 1927, Belgian astronomer Georges Lemaitre became the first to propose the theory of an expanding universe (later confirmed by Edwin Hubble). He proposed that the expanding Universe could be traced back to a special point, which he called the "primordial atom", back in time. This laid the foundation for the modern Big Bang theory.
